About CineVix & Quizify

Welcome to CineVix, where entertainment meets education. We're creating a unique space for you to learn, relax, and grow, all powered by the innovative spirit of Quizify.

Our Story

Our journey began with Quizify, a platform created by our founder, Raj Mishra, to make studying for competitive exams more engaging and effective. As students embraced this new way of learning, we realized that true success isn't just about hard work—it's also about finding balance and inspiration.

That's where CineVix comes in. It's the other half of our vision: a curated world of entertainment designed to help you unwind, recharge, and find new perspectives. We believe a powerful film can be as enlightening as a textbook, and a moment of joy is essential for a sharp mind. CineVix is your space to relax after a study session and return to your goals refreshed and motivated.

Meet the Creator

Raj Mishra, Founder & Lead Developer

A passionate software engineer with a vision to revolutionize the student experience. Raj is the architect behind both Quizify and CineVix, dedicated to building an ecosystem that supports both learning and leisure.

Our Mission

Our mission is to build a holistic platform that nurtures both the mind and the spirit. We provide top-tier educational tools through Quizify and high-quality entertainment through CineVix, creating a balanced ecosystem for personal growth. We're here to help you learn, enjoy, and succeed, all in one place.
